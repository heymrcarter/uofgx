Thank you for your support

Effective immediately Destiny Clan Manager is no longer operational.

I had a great time building DCM and I learned a lot along the way. Since the release of Shadowkeep several API integrations with the Destiny 2 API have been broken. Also in that time I've purchased my first home, taken a new job, and have several other personal responsibilities that keep me from continuing to work on the site.

Given my inability to continue to give DCM the time it demands to stay in tip top shape, I've made the difficult decision to let her go. Thank you to the great Destiny 2 community and my old clan, Unity of Guardians, who inspired me to create the site in the first place.
